Game 2048

Gameplay: Board: The game is played on a 4x4 grid. Tiles: Initially, there are two tiles on the board, each with a number 2. Movement: You can move all tiles in four directions: up, down, left, and right. All tiles on the board will slide in the chosen direction until they hit the edge of the board or another tile. Combining Tiles: When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with the sum of the two numbers. For example, if two tiles with the number 2 touch, they merge into one tile with the number 4. Scoring: Every time you combine tiles, your score increases by the value of the newly formed tile. For example, merging two tiles with the number 4 will increase your score by 4. Winning: The game is won when you create a tile with the number 2048. However, you can keep playing to achieve higher scores and larger tiles. Game Over: The game is over when there are no more possible moves left (i.e., the board is full and no adjacent tiles have the same number).
