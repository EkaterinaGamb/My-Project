Game Tic Tac Toe

Gameplay: Turns: Players take turns marking an empty cell in the grid with their respective marker (X or O). Starting Player: Decide who goes first. This can be done by a coin toss, rock-paper-scissors, or any other method agreed upon by the players. Marking Cells: On your turn, mark one of the empty cells in the grid with your marker. Each player's mark must be unique; Xs and Os are used exclusively by the player who put them on the board. Winning Conditions: The game can end in one of the following ways: Row: A player gets three of their marks in a horizontal row. Column: A player gets three of their marks in a vertical column. Diagonal: A player gets three of their marks diagonally from one corner of the grid to the opposite corner. Draw: If all cells are marked and no player has achieved a row, column, or diagonal of three marks, the game ends in a draw.
